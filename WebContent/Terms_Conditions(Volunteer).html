SCHOOL POLICIES

The Board believes in the appropriate school conducts, based on respect for oneself, respect for others and the school’s property is essential to the development of the responsible citizens. To this end, students are expected to:
  • Be aware of and obey all the school’s conduct
  • Attend classes regularly
  • Work cooperatively and attentively of their studies and with their assignments
  • Respect the rights of all people within the school including peers, staff, and parents
  • Value the school’s facilities
  • Value the importance of the ethnic diversity of our school’s community
  • Behave in a safe and responsible manner at all times
  • All electronic devices including but not limited to: cell phone, MP3, Laptop, camera and gaming device must be put away during the school hours or they will be confiscated
  • Avoid threatening, harassing, bullying, intimidating or assaulting others in any way within our school’s community
